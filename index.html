Syllabus Topics

Indian Knowledge System Overview

Introduction to the foundational principles and historical context of India's traditional knowledge systems.

Vedic Studies

Exploration of ancient Vedic texts, philosophies, and their contributions to various fields of knowledge.

Indian Mathematics and Number Systems

Study of mathematical concepts, algorithms, and numerical systems developed in ancient India.

Indian Astronomy and Calendar System

Analysis of astronomical observations, celestial calculations, and traditional calendar systems.

Metallurgy and Engineering

Examination of ancient Indian metallurgical practices, material sciences, and engineering principles.

Ancient Indian Science and Technology

Exploration of scientific discoveries, technological innovations, and their practical applications in ancient India.

Architecture and Town Planning

Study of traditional architectural principles, construction techniques, and urban planning methodologies.

Fr.Crce IKS

The Indian Knowledge System (IKS) explores India's rich heritage in science, mathematics, astronomy, metallurgy, and architecture. It covers ancient texts like the Vedas, unique number systems, and contributions to algebra and trigonometry. Topics include traditional irrigation, shipbuilding, surgical techniques, and temple architecture. This module highlights India's historical advancements and their relevance today.
